III. Network Architecture and Policies: Decisions and Tradeoffs

The requirements for your network will directly determine the way it's built (i.e. its architecture) and its behavior (i.e. its policies). Indirectly, the requirements will determine the cost of your network.

This part of the book will guide you through the architectural and policy choices that're made in building reliable Internet connectivity.
